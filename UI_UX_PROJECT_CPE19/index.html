About Us

            Welcome to the "About Us" page of our team, comprised of Jean Leanard Subida, Nyco Lawrence Ladimo, and Lorenzo Alarva, all hailing from the prestigious Laguna State Polytechnic University - Santa Cruz Campus. We are passionate students pursuing our Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering, currently enrolled in Section CPE-4A. Our journey began when we embarked on the course CPE19 - Emerging Technologies in CPE, which opened up a world of innovative opportunities. Our collective vision is to leverage these emerging technologies to create responsive webpages that not only function flawlessly but also offer an exceptional User Interface/User Experience (UI/UX) design. With a commitment to staying on the cutting edge of technology and design, we are excited to share our progress and expertise with you as we continue to grow and learn in this dynamic field. Thank you for joining us on this journey of technological exploration and innovation!
